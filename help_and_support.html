Common Issues & Solutions

Q: I'm not receiving personalized news.

A: Ensure your preferences are set correctly in the settings. Try refreshing or re-logging in.

Q: My preferences are not saving.

A: Clear your browser cache and try again.

Q: How do I report inappropriate content?

A: Click on the "Report" button next to the article.
